Sea Dragon

The Sea Dragon is the largest chemical rocket ever designed matched only by the Interplanetary Transport System (ITS the predecessor to the SpaceX Starship). The Sea Dragon could lift 550 Tonnes to LEO (low earth orbit). For reference, the Saturn V could lift 118 Tonnes to LEO.
